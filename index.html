DEPARTMENT OF ENERGY

Case Study

Executive Summary: I re-designed the homepage of the Dept. of Energy website to make it more user friendly and useful for us and them.
Project Overview: We were tasked with re designing a goverment agency website. We chose the DOE becuase they had poor accessibiltiy, bad naviagtion and poor Information Architecture.

    Problem Statement: The Department of Energy website does not supply users with an understanding of who they are and what they do. This site also does not offer clear and helpful navigation to pages that can help users save money or gain information on tax credits, energy efficient building techniques and many other useful resources that the DOE supplies for the public.
    The Goal: Our goal was to focus on the Homepage Layout as well as re-imagine the navigation throughout the site. In this iterated design we set out to supply the user with better information to understand the Department of Energy Mission and how you can save money through their resources.
